# Top-Candidate Shortlist — First Ten Screens

## Executive summary
Across the first ten real screened sites, the MVP now has a clear top tier.

The current shortlist for highest-priority follow-up is:
1. **16 Mons Road, Buderim**
2. **39 Park Rd, Nambour**
3. **18A Quambi Place, Buderim**

These three sites stand above the rest because they combine either:
- stronger raw landholding scale
- stronger explicit redevelopment signal
- or cleaner land-only opportunity structure

The remaining seven sites are still useful, but they currently sit in the secondary pool.

## What separates the top 3 from the rest
### 1. They offer stronger raw strategic value
- 16 Mons Road and 18A Quambi Place stand out on parcel scale
- 39 Park Rd stands out on public redevelopment signal

### 2. They are more likely to justify deeper work
Even with unresolved planning fields, they are clearly worth spending verification effort on.

### 3. They are better tests of the MVP’s commercial usefulness
If the workflow cannot materially improve confidence on these three, the MVP will struggle to justify itself as a rapid screening product.
