# Guardrails and Disclaimers

## Scope guardrails
- Sunshine Coast LGA only.
- Residential subdivision only.
- MVP is a screening tool, not a planning determination engine.
- No expansion into finance, architecture, civil design, surveying, or legal advice in Phase 1.

## Evidence guardrails
- Do not state unverified planning controls as definitive.
- Always mark placeholders where verified references are still needed.
- Preserve source references and verification flags.
- Human review remains mandatory for Phase 1 and Phase 2 final outputs.

## Commercial guardrails
- Optimise for screening usefulness, not product completeness.
- Avoid workflow steps that materially increase per-site assessment time without clear value.
- Prefer simple repeatable rules over fragile sophistication.

## Disclaimer language
Suggested standing disclaimer:

> This output is an early-stage screening assessment only. It is not a substitute for formal town planning, surveying, engineering, legal, servicing, valuation, or development approval advice. Any zoning interpretation, overlay impact, minimum lot size requirement, yield assumption, application history implication, or physical constraint conclusion must be independently verified from authoritative sources before relying on the assessment for acquisition, development, or client advice.
